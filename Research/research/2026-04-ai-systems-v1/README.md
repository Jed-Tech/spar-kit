# AI systems for v1 (March-April 2026)

## Purpose

Capture the March-April 2026 research behind the question from `docs/ROADMAP.md`:

> What should count as "other AI systems" for a practical v1 release after Cursor, Codex, and Claude Code?

This folder records the evidence, tradeoffs, and final recommendation so the roadmap decision is easy to revisit later.

## Files

- `research.md` - detailed market and implementation-fit notes
- `summary.md` - short recommendation and rationale
